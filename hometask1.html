ISRO SATELLITE

Posted on 26 March, 2023

ISRO's heaviest rocket LVM3 carrying 36 satellites belonging to UK-based OneWeb Group lifted off from this space port on Sunday. The second dedicated mission for NewSpace India Ltd, the commercial arm of Indian Space Research Organisation is part of the agreement signed with Network Access Associates Ltd, UK.

Nikhat Zareen beats Vietnam's Nguyen Thi Tam to bag second World Championships gold

Posted on 26 March, 2023

Nikhat Zareen produced a strong show against Vietnam's Nguyen Thi Tam to win gold in the 48-50 kg finals at the IBA Women's World Boxing Championships in New Delhi on Sunday. The Indian pugilist secured a unanimous decision win against Thi Tam, who is the first Vietnamese boxer to reach a World Championship final.

Magnitude 5.5 earthquake strikes Tarapaca, Chile region

Posted on 25 March, 2023

A magnitude 5.5 earthquake struck the Tarapaca region in Chile on Saturday, the European Mediterranean Seismological Centre said. The quake was at a depth of 155 kilometers (96.31 miles) below the earth's surface.
